Who We Are?

Nike, Inc. is a global leader in sportswear and athletic gear, dedicated to inspiring athletes of all levels to achieve their potential. Founded in 1964, Nike designs, develops, and markets footwear, apparel, and equipment that combine innovative technology with cutting-edge style. Our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world, with the belief that if you have a body, you are an athlete.

NIKE, Inc. is a team comprised of the Nike, Jordan, and Converse brands driven by a shared purpose to leave an enduring impact.
